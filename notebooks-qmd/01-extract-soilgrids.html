Bounding boxes

  • The bounding boxes for each analyzed country are set to a list, in this case: Japan, United States, Canada, Switzerland, and South Korea.
  • The limits for each country were taken from the following resource on Github.
  • Also useful was the bboxfinder.com

Extraction by country

  • The following are the ten soil variables obtained from SoilGrids. In all of them the averages for the depth of 0-5 cm were extracted, except in the variable ocs which is only available for depth 30 cm.
    • bdod: Bulk density
    • cec: Cation exchange capacity at ph 7
    • cfvo: Coarse fragments volumetric
    • clay: Clay content
    • nitrogen: Nitrogen
    • phh2o: Soil pH in H2O
    • sand: Sand content
    • silt: Silt content
    • soc: Soil organic carbon content
    • ocd: Organic carbon densities
    • ocs: Soil organic carbon stock (depth of 30cm)
Code 
# Inputs
variables = ["bdod", "cec", "cfvo", "clay", "nitrogen", "phh2o", "sand",
             "silt", "soc", "ocd", "ocs"]
resolution = 0.05
path_export = "../external-data/soilgrids/"
